In our editorial launching the new Reproducible Research Track in CiSE \citep*{Barba_2017} , we promised to explore a few innovations to the peer-review process. Because we require articles submitted to this track to adhere to practices that safeguard reproducibility, we must review for these aspects deliberately. A reproducibility reviewer (or one of the track editors) will be charged with checking availability, quality and of digital artifacts (data, code)
